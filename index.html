Frequently Asked Questions

Is my medical data secure with IgeA?

Yes. IgeA uses local-first architecture with end-to-end encryption. Your data stays on your device by default and you maintain complete control over your information.

Which medical data sources can I connect to IgeA?

IgeA supports importing data from major electronic health record systems, popular health apps, wearable devices, and manual data entry for complete flexibility.

Can I share my health data with my doctor?

Yes. IgeA allows you to generate comprehensive reports that can be shared with healthcare providers through secure channels or exported as PDFs.

Do I need technical knowledge to use IgeA?

No. IgeA is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. The interface guides you through setting up data sources and creating visualizations.
